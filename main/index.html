CURRENT SCENARIO

A.I is becoming a buzzword for the student community.

A lot of time and energy is being spent on learning libraries instead of working on fundamentals, asking questions and trying to learn more

Therefore, there is a lack of culture based on useful applications, research and healthy discussions.

There is a lack of guidance for students getting into A.I and unrealistic expectations from the field.

Lack of communication between the A.I industry, A.I researchers and student community.
